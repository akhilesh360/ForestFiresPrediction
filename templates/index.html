Welcome to Algeria Wild Fires Prediction

Algerian wildfires are fueled by climate change, drought, hot winds, and human factors. Dense vegetation exacerbates the fires, causing significant damage. Recent advancements in firefighting and public awareness have reduced the impact, with no fatalities reported in 2024.

A predictive model helps anticipate wildfire risks by using key factors - temperature (Â°C), relative humidity (RH %), wind speed (Ws in km/h), rainfall (mm) and others. This data-driven approach supports timely interventions and resource allocation to mitigate wildfire impacts.

  • Temperature: The temperature in degrees Celsius.
  • RH: Relative Humidity in percentage.
  • Ws: Wind speed in km/h.
  • Rain: Rainfall in mm.
  • FFMC: Fine Fuel Moisture Code.
  • DMC: Duff Moisture Code.
  • ISI: Initial Spread Index.
  • Classes: Fire weather index classes.
  • Region: The region in Algeria.
